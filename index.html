Frequently Asked Questions

You’ve got questions, we’ve got answers.

What is SunTrader?

SunTrader is a leading stock broker in India, offering a comprehensive platform for trading in commodities, futures, and options. We provide secure, reliable, and user-friendly services tailored to meet the needs of both new and experienced traders.

What are the brokerage charges?

Our brokerage charges are competitive and transparent. We offer:

  • Derivatives Futures and Options: ₹0 per executed order
  • Commodities and Currencies: ₹0 per executed order

SunTrader is offering 0 brokerage charges on assets and all exchanges.

How do I open an account?

Opening an account with SunTrader is simple and can be done online. All you need is a Phone number and email Address, that’s it! No other documents required. The account opening process typically takes just a few seconds.

What is the minimum Deposit?

A Beginner trader can start trading with as low as Rs.500 as well, however a bigger capital reduces the Risk on the account.

How do I deposit funds?

You can deposit funds into your SunTrader account through multiple methods, including:

  • Net Banking
  • UPI
  • NEFT/RTGS
  • IMPS

Deposits are usually processed instantly or within a minutes, depending on the method used.

How can I withdraw funds?

Withdrawing funds is easy via our platform:

  1. Log in to your SunTrader account.
  2. Navigate to the “Withdrawals section”
  3. Click on “New Request”
  4. Enter the amount, bank details and submit.

Withdrawals are typically processed Instantly or within 2 hours.

What trading platforms are offered?

SunTrader offers multiple trading platforms to suit your needs:

  • Web Platform: Trade from any browser with our intuitive web-based platform.
  • Mobile App: Trade on the go with our highly-rated mobile app available on Android and iOS.
  • Desktop Application: For advanced traders, we provide a desktop trading application with comprehensive charting tools.

Do you charge deposit/withdrawal fees?

No, not at all. We are committed to providing the best trading solutions for our traders, so we do not charge any deposit or withdrawal fees.
